# Mind Mapp — Product FAQ

Q: What is Mind Mapp?
A: A lightweight, keyboard-first mind map app built for speed and clarity.

Q: How do I add nodes?
A: Enter creates a sibling; Tab creates a child; Shift+Tab promotes a node.

Q: How do I edit text?
A: Double-click a node or press E to enter edit mode.

Q: How do I search?
A: Cmd/Ctrl+K opens search.

Q: How do I export?
A: Cmd/Ctrl+S exports JSON; Cmd/Ctrl+Shift+S exports PNG.

Q: How do I pan/zoom?
A: Shift+drag pans; Ctrl/Cmd+wheel zooms; double-click background resets view.

Q: Is autosave enabled?
A: Yes — changes autosave to localStorage with a 600ms debounce.

Q: Where is my data stored?
A: In browser localStorage under the key `mindmapp.v0.1.map`.

Q: How do I reset view?
A: Double-click the background or use the Reset View toolbar button.
